Cable and DSL operators are observing your surfing habits.

Cable One last fall conducted a six-month trial of a network-based technology that tracks consumers' Internet movements in an effort to amass refined data on Web-surfer habits that can be sold to advertisers at premium rates. MultiChannel.com, Aug 2008.

I cannot say that this is not valuable information to our for-profit ISPs.  However, the data collected is no way relevant to helping them perform the services they are contracted for: providing a connection to the Internet.
